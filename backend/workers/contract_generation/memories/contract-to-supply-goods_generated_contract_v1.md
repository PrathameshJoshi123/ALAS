## **Background**

- **(A)** The Distributor is engaged in the distribution of electronic components and requires a reliable supply of microchips and circuit boards.

- **(B)** The Supplier is a manufacturer of electronic components, including microchips and circuit boards, and is capable of supplying the required goods.

- **(C)** The parties have agreed to enter into this Contract for the supply of electronic components on the terms and conditions set out herein.

## **Recitals**

**IT IS HEREBY AGREED** between the Supplier and the Distributor that:

1. The Supplier shall supply the electronic components as specified in this Contract, and the Distributor shall pay the Supplier the Contract Sum in accordance with the terms of this Contract.

2. This Contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in respect of the supply of electronic components.

## **1. Definitions and Scope**

### **1.1 Definitions**

- **"Contract Period"**: The period from the Commencement Date to the Termination Date, as specified in this Contract.
- **"Commencement Date"**: The date on which this Contract comes into effect.
- **"Termination Date"**: The date on which this Contract terminates, as specified in Clause 10.
- **"Goods"**: Electronic components, including microchips and circuit boards, as specified in the Specification.
- **"Specification"**: The detailed description of the Goods to be supplied, including quality standards, technical specifications, and packaging requirements.
- **"Contract Sum"**: The total amount payable by the Distributor to the Supplier for the supply of the Goods, as specified in Clause 5.
- **"Delivery Date"**: The date by which the Supplier must deliver the Goods to the Distributor, as specified in Clause 4.
- **"Force Majeure Event"**: Any event beyond the reasonable control of either party, including but not limited to natural disasters, war, civil unrest, or government actions.
- **"Inspection Period"**: The period within which the Distributor may inspect the Goods upon delivery, as specified in Clause 6.

### **1.2 Scope of the Contract**

This Contract governs the supply of electronic components by the Supplier to the Distributor. The Supplier shall supply the Goods in accordance with the Specification, and the Distributor shall pay the Contract Sum as per the terms of this Contract.
