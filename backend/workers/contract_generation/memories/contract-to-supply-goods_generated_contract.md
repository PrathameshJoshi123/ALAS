# CONTRACT TO SUPPLY ELECTRONIC COMPONENTS

## **Parties**

**BETWEEN**

- **(1) [Insert Name of Manufacturing Company]**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at [Insert Address] (hereinafter referred to as the **"Supplier")**

- **(2) [Insert Name of Distributor]**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at [Insert Address] (hereinafter referred to as the **"Distributor")**

## **Recitals**

- **(A)** The Distributor is engaged in the distribution of electronic components and requires a reliable supply of such components to meet its business needs.

- **(B)** The Supplier is a manufacturer of electronic components, including microchips and circuit boards, and is capable of supplying the required goods to the Distributor.

- **(C)** The parties have agreed to enter into this Contract to formalize the terms and conditions for the supply of electronic components by the Supplier to the Distributor.

## **IT IS HEREBY AGREED** between the Supplier and the Distributor that:

1. This Contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties in respect of the supply of electronic components.
2. The Supplier shall supply the electronic components in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Contract, and the Distributor shall pay the Supplier the Contract Sum in accordance with the terms of this Contract.

## **1. Definitions and Scope**

### **1.1 Definitions**
In this Contract, the following terms shall have the meanings ascribed to them:

- **"Contract"**: This agreement, including all schedules, annexures, and amendments.
- **"Contract Sum"**: The total amount payable by the Distributor to the Supplier for the supply of electronic components, as detailed in **Section 5 (Schedule of Prices)**.
- **"Delivery Date"**: The date on which the electronic components are to be delivered by the Supplier to the Distributor, as specified in the order.
- **"Electronic Components"**: Microchips, circuit boards, and other related components as specified in **Section 4 (Specification)**.
- **"Force Majeure Event"**: Any event beyond the reasonable control of either party, including but not limited to acts of God, war, riot, civil commotion, terrorist acts, natural disasters, or government actions.
- **"Guarantee Period"**: A period of 18 months from the date of delivery, during which the Supplier guarantees the electronic components against defects in materials and workmanship.
- **"Order"**: A purchase order issued by the Distributor to the Supplier for the supply of electronic components.
- **"Parties"**: The Supplier and the Distributor collectively.
- **"Price Revision Date"**: The date on which the price of the electronic components may be revised, occurring every 6 months from the Commencement Date.
- **"Termination Date"**: The date on which this Contract terminates, either by mutual agreement, notice, or breach.

### **1.2 Scope**
This Contract governs the supply of electronic components by the Supplier to the Distributor, including the terms of delivery, payment, inspection, warranty, and termination.

## **2. Obligations of the Supplier**

### **2.1 Supply of Electronic Components**
- The Supplier shall supply the electronic components in accordance with the specifications, quantities, and delivery schedules set out in **Section 4 (Specification)** and the Orders issued by the Distributor.
- The Supplier shall ensure that the electronic components comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.

### **2.2 Delivery**
- The Supplier shall deliver the electronic components to the Distributor’s designated premises within **7 days** of the date of the Order.
- The Supplier shall provide the Distributor with an advice note specifying the means of transport, the place and date of dispatch, the number of packages, and their weight and volume.
- Time of delivery shall be of the essence. If the Supplier fails to deliver the electronic components within the agreed time, the Distributor may, without prejudice to any other rights and remedies, terminate the Contract or claim compensation for delay as specified in **Clause 6.3 (Penalty for Delay)**.

### **2.3 Inspection and Acceptance**
- The Distributor or its authorized representatives may inspect the electronic components at the Supplier’s premises during manufacture or after delivery.
- The Distributor may reject any electronic components that fail to conform to the specifications or are defective. The Supplier shall, at its own expense, repair or replace such components within **5 Working Days** of receiving notice of rejection.
- The Distributor shall be deemed to have accepted the electronic components if it does not notify the Supplier of any defects or non-conformities within **10 Working Days** of delivery.

### **2.4 Warranty**
- The Supplier guarantees the electronic components for a period of **18 months** from the date of delivery against faulty materials or workmanship.
- If the Distributor notifies the Supplier of any defects within the Guarantee Period, the Supplier shall promptly remedy such defects by repair or replacement at no additional cost to the Distributor.

## **3. Obligations of the Distributor**

### **3.1 Payment**
- The Distributor shall pay the Supplier the Contract Sum for the electronic components supplied, in accordance with the pricing and payment terms set out in **Section 5 (Schedule of Prices)**.
- The Distributor shall make payment within **30 days** of the date of the Supplier’s invoice, provided the electronic components have been delivered and accepted.

### **3.2 Orders**
- The Distributor shall place Orders for the electronic components in writing, specifying the quantity, type, and delivery date.
- The Distributor shall not cancel or modify an Order without the prior written consent of the Supplier.

### **3.3 Inspection**
- The Distributor shall inspect the electronic components upon delivery and notify the Supplier of any defects or non-conformities within **10 Working Days** of delivery.

## **4. Commercial Terms**

### **4.1 Pricing**
- The price of the electronic components shall be **₹50 per unit**, subject to revision every **6 months** from the Commencement Date based on changes in the cost of raw materials.
- The Supplier shall notify the Distributor in writing of any proposed price revision at least **30 days** before the Price Revision Date. The parties shall negotiate in good faith to agree on the revised price.

### **4.2 Minimum Order Quantity**
- The Distributor shall place Orders for a minimum of **10,000 units per month**.

### **4.3 Invoicing**
- The Supplier shall submit invoices to the Distributor within **5 Working Days** of delivery of the electronic components.
- Invoices shall include a detailed breakdown of the electronic components supplied, quantities, unit prices, and the total amount due.

### **4.4 Taxes**
- All taxes, duties, and levies applicable to the supply of electronic components shall be borne by the Distributor, in addition to the Contract Sum.

## **5. Legal Clauses**

### **5.1 Confidentiality**
- The parties shall keep confidential all information disclosed by the other party in connection with this Contract and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party.
- This obligation of confidentiality shall survive the termination of this Contract.

### **5.2 Intellectual Property**
- All intellectual property rights in the electronic components and any related documentation shall remain the property of the Supplier.
- The Distributor shall not reproduce, modify, or distribute the electronic components or any related documentation without the prior written consent of the Supplier.

### **5.3 Limitation of Liability**
- The Supplier shall not be liable for any indirect, consequential, or incidental damages arising out of or in connection with this Contract.
- The Supplier’s total liability under this Contract shall not exceed the total Contract Sum paid by the Distributor for the electronic components supplied during the **12 months** preceding the date of the claim.

### **5.4 Force Majeure**
- Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this Contract due to a Force Majeure Event.
- If a Force Majeure Event prevents either party from performing its obligations for a continuous period of **30 days**, the other party may terminate this Contract by giving **15 days’ written notice**.

### **5.5 Indemnification**
- The Supplier shall indemnify and hold harmless the Distributor from and against any claims, damages, or liabilities arising out of the Supplier’s breach of this Contract or negligence.
- The Distributor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Supplier from and against any claims, damages, or liabilities arising out of the Distributor’s breach of this Contract or negligence.

## **6. Penalty for Delay and Termination**

### **6.1 Penalty for Delay**
- If the Supplier fails to deliver the electronic components within the agreed time, the Distributor may claim compensation for delay at the rate of **0.5% of the value of the delayed electronic components per week**, up to a maximum of **5% of the total value of the delayed electronic components**.

### **6.2 Termination for Convenience**
- Either party may terminate this Contract by giving **60 days’ written notice** to the other party.

### **6.3 Termination for Breach**
- Either party may terminate this Contract immediately by written notice if the other party:
  - Materially breaches any term of this Contract and fails to remedy such breach within **30 days** of receiving written notice;
  - Becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation, or is unable to pay its debts as they fall due;
  - Commits an act of fraud or misrepresentation in connection with this Contract.

### **6.4 Effect of Termination**
- Upon termination, the Supplier shall deliver all outstanding electronic components to the Distributor, and the Distributor shall pay for all electronic components delivered up to the Termination Date.
- Neither party shall have any further obligations to the other under this Contract, except for those that survive termination, such as confidentiality and indemnification.

## **7. Dispute Resolution and Governing Law**

### **7.1 Dispute Resolution**
- Any disputes or differences arising out of or in connection with this Contract shall first be referred to the respective representatives of the parties for resolution.
- If the representatives are unable to resolve the dispute within **14 days**, the dispute shall be referred to the senior management of both parties for resolution.
- If the senior management is unable to resolve the dispute within **14 days**, the dispute shall be referred to arbitration.

### **7.2 Arbitration**
- Any dispute that cannot be resolved amicably shall be referred to arbitration in **Mumbai**, India, in accordance with the **Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996**.
- The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator appointed by mutual agreement of the parties. If the parties cannot agree on an arbitrator, the arbitrator shall be appointed by the **Mumbai Centre for International Arbitration (MCIA)**.
- The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on the parties.

### **7.3 Governing Law**
- This Contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **India**.
- The courts in **Mumbai** shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Contract.

## **8. Term and Termination**

### **8.1 Contract Period**
- This Contract shall commence on the date of signing and shall remain in effect for a period of **2 years** (the **"Contract Period"**).

### **8.2 Renewal**
- The parties may renew this Contract for an additional period by mutual agreement, subject to the terms and conditions to be agreed upon at the time of renewal.

## **9. Miscellaneous**

### **9.1 Notices**
- All notices and communications under this Contract shall be in writing and delivered by hand, registered post, or email to the addresses specified in this Contract.

### **9.2 Assignment**
- Neither party shall assign or transfer its rights or obligations under this Contract without the prior written consent of the other party.

### **9.3 Waiver**
- The failure of either party to enforce any provision of this Contract shall not be construed as a waiver of such provision or the right to enforce it.

### **9.4 Entire Agreement**
- This Contract constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and representations.

### **9.5 Amendments**
- Any amendments to this Contract must be in writing and signed by both parties.
