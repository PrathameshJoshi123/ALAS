## **IT IS HEREBY AGREED** between the Supplier and the Distributor that:

## **1. DEFINITIONS AND SCOPE**

### **1.1 Definitions**
In this Agreement, the following terms shall have the meanings assigned to them:

- **"Agreement"**: This Supply Agreement, including all schedules, annexures, and amendments.
- **"Commencement Date"**: The date of execution of this Agreement.
- **"Contract Period"**: A period of 2 (two) years from the Commencement Date, unless terminated earlier as per the terms of this Agreement.
- **"Delivery"**: The act of transferring the Goods from the Supplier to the Distributor as per the terms of this Agreement.
- **"Distributor"**: [Insert Distributor Name], as defined in the Parties section.
- **"Force Majeure Event"**: Any event beyond the reasonable control of either party, including but not limited to acts of God, war, riots, strikes, or governmental actions.
- **"Goods"**: Electronic components, including microchips and circuit boards, as specified in **Schedule A** of this Agreement.
- **"Inspection"**: The process of examining the Goods by the Distributor to ensure compliance with the specifications and quality standards.
- **"Order"**: A purchase order issued by the Distributor to the Supplier for the supply of Goods.
- **"Price"**: The price per unit of the Goods, as specified in **Section 4 (Commercial Terms)** of this Agreement.
- **"Quality Standards"**: The specifications and standards for the Goods, as detailed in **Schedule A** of this Agreement.
- **"Supplier"**: Acme Manufacturing Company, as defined in the Parties section.
- **"Termination Date"**: The date on which this Agreement is terminated as per the terms herein.

### **1.2 Scope of Agreement**
This Agreement governs the supply of electronic components by the Supplier to the Distributor. The Supplier shall supply the Goods in accordance with the terms and conditions set forth in this Agreement, and the Distributor shall accept and pay for the Goods as specified herein.

### **1.3 Applicable Law**
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Mumbai under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

## **2. OBLIGATIONS OF THE SUPPLIER**

### **2.1 Supply of Goods**
The Supplier shall supply the Goods to the Distributor in accordance with the specifications, quantity, and quality standards set forth in **Schedule A** of this Agreement. The Supplier shall ensure that the Goods conform to the agreed specifications and are free from defects.

### **2.2 Delivery**
The Supplier shall deliver the Goods to the Distributor within 7 (seven) days from the date of receipt of the Order. Delivery shall be made to the address specified in the Order or as otherwise agreed in writing by the parties.

### **2.3 Quality Assurance**
The Supplier shall ensure that the Goods meet the Quality Standards specified in **Schedule A**. The Supplier shall provide all necessary documentation, including test reports and certificates of compliance, to verify the quality of the Goods.

### **2.4 Packaging and Labeling**
The Supplier shall package and label the Goods in accordance with industry standards and any specific instructions provided by the Distributor. The packaging shall be sufficient to protect the Goods during transit and storage.

### **2.5 Notification of Defects**
In the event the Supplier becomes aware of any defects or non-conformities in the Goods after delivery, the Supplier shall immediately notify the Distributor and take corrective action at its own expense.

## **3. OBLIGATIONS OF THE DISTRIBUTOR**

### **3.1 Placement of Orders**
The Distributor shall place Orders for the Goods in writing, specifying the quantity, delivery address, and any other relevant details. The minimum order quantity shall be 10,000 (ten thousand) units per month.

### **3.2 Inspection and Acceptance**
The Distributor shall inspect the Goods upon delivery to ensure compliance with the specifications and Quality Standards. The Distributor shall notify the Supplier of any defects or non-conformities within 5 (five) days of delivery. Failure to notify the Supplier within this period shall constitute acceptance of the Goods.

### **3.3 Payment**
The Distributor shall pay the Supplier the agreed Price for the Goods within 30 (thirty) days from the date of receipt of the invoice. Payment shall be made via bank transfer or as otherwise agreed in writing by the parties.

### **3.4 Confidentiality**
The Distributor shall maintain the confidentiality of any proprietary or sensitive information shared by the Supplier in connection with this Agreement and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Supplier.

## **4. COMMERCIAL TERMS**

### **4.1 Pricing**
The Price for the Goods shall be ₹50 (Indian Rupees Fifty) per unit, subject to revision every 6 (six) months based on changes in the cost of raw materials. Any revision in the Price shall be mutually agreed upon in writing by both parties.

### **4.2 Payment Terms**
Payment shall be made within 30 (thirty) days from the date of receipt of the invoice. The Supplier shall issue an invoice for each delivery of Goods, and the Distributor shall verify the invoice for accuracy before making payment.

### **4.3 Minimum Order Quantity**
The minimum order quantity for the Goods shall be 10,000 (ten thousand) units per month. The Distributor may place additional orders beyond the minimum quantity, subject to the Supplier’s acceptance.

### **4.4 Delivery Charges**
Delivery charges, if any, shall be borne by the Supplier unless otherwise agreed in writing. The Supplier shall include all applicable taxes and duties in the Price.

### **4.5 Price Adjustments**
In the event of significant changes in the cost of raw materials or other factors affecting the production of the Goods, the Supplier may request a revision of the Price. Any such revision shall be subject to mutual agreement and documented in writing.

## **5. QUALITY STANDARDS AND INSPECTION**

### **5.1 Quality Standards**
The Goods supplied under this Agreement shall conform to the quality standards specified in **Schedule A**. The Supplier shall ensure that the Goods meet all applicable industry standards and regulatory requirements.

### **5.2 Inspection**
The Distributor shall have the right to inspect the Goods upon delivery to verify compliance with the quality standards. The Distributor may also conduct inspections at the Supplier’s premises during the manufacturing process, provided that reasonable notice is given.

### **5.3 Rejection of Goods**
If the Goods fail to meet the quality standards, the Distributor may reject the Goods and notify the Supplier within 5 (five) days of delivery. The Supplier shall, at its own expense, replace or repair the defective Goods or refund the Price paid by the Distributor.

### **5.4 Warranty**
The Supplier warrants that the Goods shall be free from defects in materials and workmanship for a period of 18 (eighteen) months from the date of delivery. In the event of any defects, the Supplier shall, at its option, repair or replace the defective Goods at no additional cost to the Distributor.

## **6. TERM AND TERMINATION**

### **6.1 Term**
This Agreement shall commence on the Commencement Date and shall remain in effect for a period of 2 (two) years, unless terminated earlier as per the terms of this Agreement.

### **6.2 Termination for Convenience**
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing 60 (sixty) days’ written notice to the other party. Such termination shall not affect any rights or obligations accrued prior to the effective date of termination.

### **6.3 Termination for Cause**
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice to the other party in the event of a material breach of this Agreement by the other party. A material breach shall include, but not be limited to, failure to deliver the Goods, failure to pay for the Goods, or any other breach that substantially affects the rights or obligations of the parties.

### **6.4 Effect of Termination**
Upon termination of this Agreement, the Supplier shall deliver any outstanding Orders, and the Distributor shall pay for all Goods delivered prior to the Termination Date. Any disputes arising from the termination shall be resolved in accordance with the dispute resolution provisions of this Agreement.

## **7. CONFIDENTIALITY AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY**

### **7.1 Confidentiality**
Both parties shall maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary and sensitive information shared during the term of this Agreement. Neither party shall disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party.

### **7.2 Intellectual Property**
All intellectual property rights, including but not limited to patents, trademarks, and copyrights, related to the Goods shall remain the property of the Supplier. The Distributor shall not use, reproduce, or distribute any intellectual property of the Supplier without prior written consent.

## **8. INDEMNIFICATION AND LIABILITY**

### **8.1 Indemnification by Supplier**
The Supplier shall indemnify and hold harmless the Distributor from and against any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising out of or in connection with:

- Any defects in the Goods supplied under this Agreement.
- Any breach of warranty or representation made by the Supplier.
- Any failure of the Goods to comply with applicable laws or regulations.

### **8.2 Indemnification by Distributor**
The Distributor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Supplier from and against any and all claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising out of or in connection with:

- Any misuse or improper handling of the Goods by the Distributor.
- Any breach of this Agreement by the Distributor.

### **8.3 Limitation of Liability**
Neither party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising out of or in connection with this Agreement. The total liability of either party under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid by the Distributor for the Goods during the 12 (twelve) months preceding the date of the claim.

## **9. FORCE MAJEURE**

### **9.1 Definition**
A Force Majeure Event shall include any event beyond the reasonable control of either party, including but not limited to acts of God, war, riots, strikes, governmental actions, or natural disasters.

### **9.2 Effect of Force Majeure**
If either party is unable to perform its obligations under this Agreement due to a Force Majeure Event, such party shall notify the other party in writing within 7 (seven) days of the occurrence of the event. The affected party shall be excused from performance for the duration of the Force Majeure Event, provided that such party takes reasonable steps to mitigate the effects of the event.

### **9.3 Termination for Force Majeure**
If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than 30 (thirty) days, either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice to the other party.

## **10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION AND GOVERNING LAW**

### **10.1 Governing Law**
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

### **10.2 Dispute Resolution**
Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Mumbai under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator appointed by mutual agreement of the parties. The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on both parties.

### **10.3 Jurisdiction**
The courts in Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement.

## **11. GENERAL PROVISIONS**

### **11.1 Entire Agreement**
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding the supply of electronic components and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, or representations.

### **11.2 Amendments**
Any amendments to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both parties.

### **11.3 Notices**
All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, registered mail, or email to the addresses specified in this Agreement. Notices shall be deemed received upon delivery or, in the case of email, upon confirmation of receipt.

### **11.4 Waiver**
No waiver of any provision of this Agreement shall be effective unless in writing and signed by the party against whom the waiver is sought. A waiver of any breach shall not constitute a waiver of any other breach.

### **11.5 Assignment**
Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party.
